I. 引言

1. 期望与中位数的另一种定义:

$
\min\limits_{b}E(X-b)^2\Rightarrow E(X)=b
$

$\min\limits_bE\ |X-b|\Rightarrow med(X)=b$
$\quad$
$proof.$

$E(X-b)^2=E(X^2-2bX+b^2)=E(X^2)-2bE(X)+b^2\quad (1)$

$\ \quad$可将 $(1)$ 式看为 $b$ 的一个一元二次函数,现求 $b$ 的最小值。可由公式得：$b=E(X).$

$\ \quad$对于中位数，我们只考虑连续的情况，离散时类似可证。令随机变量 $X$ 的概率密度为 $p(x)$, 概率分布为 $F(X)$ ，则有等式成立：

$E |X-b|=\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}|x-b|\ p(x)dx$
$\qquad\qquad\ =\int_{-\infty}^{b}(b-x)p(x)dx+\int_{b}^{\infty}(x-b)p(x)dx$

$\ \quad$现要求 $b$ 的最小值，两边对 $b$ 求导，并令导数为 $0$ .

$\Rightarrow F(b)-(1-F(b))=0$ , 即 $F(b)=\dfrac{1}{2},b=med(X).$

$\quad$

2. Conditional Expectation :
i.) Law of Total Expectation :
ii.) Law of Total Variance

$proof.$

$\ \quad$设随机变量 $X,Y$的联合密度为$f(x,y)$,边缘密度分别为$f_X(x),f_Y(y)$,则成立等式：

i.) $E(X)=\int xf_X(x)dx$
$\qquad\quad\ \ \ =\int\int xf(x,y)dxdy$
$\qquad\quad\ \ \ =\int\int x\ [\ f(x|y)\cdot f_Y(y)\ ]\ dx\ dy$
$\qquad\quad\ \ \ =\int[\int x\cdot f(x|y)\ dx]\ f_Y(y)\ dy$
$\qquad\quad\ \ \ =\int E(X|y)\cdot f_Y(y)\ dy=E[E(X|Y)].$

ii.)$Var(X)=E(X-E(X))^2$
$\qquad\qquad\ \ =E[X-E(X|Y)+E(X|Y)-E(X)]^2$
$\qquad\qquad\ \ =E[X-E(X|Y)]^2+E[E(X|Y)-E(X)]^2+2E[(X-E(X|Y))(E(X|Y)-E(X))]\quad\quad (1)$

$\quad$

而$E[X-E(X|Y)]^2$
$=E\begin{Bmatrix}E[(X-E(X|Y))^2|Y)]\end{Bmatrix}$
$=E\begin{Bmatrix}E[(X^2+(E(X|Y))^2-2XE(X|Y))|Y]\end{Bmatrix}$
$=E\begin{Bmatrix}E(X^2|Y)+(E(X|Y))^2-2(E(X|Y))^2\end{Bmatrix}$
$=E\begin{Bmatrix}E(X^2|Y)-(E(X|Y))^2\end{Bmatrix}$
$=E[Var(X|Y)]$

$\quad$
$E[E(X|Y)-E(X)]^2=E\begin{Bmatrix}E(X|Y)-E[E(X|Y)]\end{Bmatrix}^2$
$\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\quad\ =Var[E(X|Y)]$

$\quad$

$E[(X-E(X|Y))(E(X|Y)-E(X))]$
$=E\begin{Bmatrix}E[(X-E(X|Y))(E(X|Y)-E(X))]|Y\end{Bmatrix}$
$=(E(X|Y))^2-E(X)E(X|Y)-(E(X|Y))^2+E(X|Y)E(X)$
$=0$

$\ \quad$所以 $(1)=Var(E(X|Y))+E(Var(X|Y)).$

iii. ) $\hat{\beta_0},\hat{\beta_1}$ 的方差 :

$Var(\hat{\beta_1})$
$=Var(\sum\limits_{j=1}^n\dfrac{(x_j-\bar{x})}{\sum\limits_{i=1}^n(x_i-\bar{x})^2}y_j)$
$=\sum\limits_{j=1}^n(\dfrac{(x_j-\bar{x})}{\sum\limits_{i=1}^n(x_i-\bar{x})^2})^2Var(y_j)\qquad(Markov-Gauss\ Condition)$
$=\dfrac{\sum\limits_{j=1}^n(x_j-\bar{x})^2}{(\sum\limits_{i=1}^n(x_i-\bar{x})^2)^2}\sigma^2$
$=\dfrac{\sigma^2}{\sum\limits_{i=1}^n(x_i-\bar{x})^2}$

$Var(\hat{\beta_0})$
$=Var(\sum\limits_{j=1}^n(\dfrac{1}{n}-\dfrac{(x_j-\bar{x})\bar{x}}{\sum\limits_{i=1}^n(x_i-\bar{x})^2})y_j)$
$=\sum\limits_{j=1}^n(\dfrac{1}{n}-\dfrac{(x_j-\bar{x})\bar{x}}{\sum\limits_{i=1}^n(x_i-\bar{x})^2})^2Var(y_i)\quad (Markov-Gauss\ Condition)$
$=\sum(\dfrac{1}{n^2}+(\dfrac{(x_j-\bar{x})\bar{x}}{\sum\limits_{i=1}^n(x_i-\bar{x})^2})^2-\dfrac{2}{n}\dfrac{(x_j-\bar{x})\bar{x}}{\sum\limits_{i=1}^n(x_i-\bar{x})^2})\sigma^2$
$=\sigma^2(\sum\dfrac{1}{n^2}+\dfrac{\bar{x}^2}{\sum\limits_{i=1}^n(x_i-\bar{x})^2}-\dfrac{2\bar{x}}{n}\sum\dfrac{(x_j-\bar{x})}{\sum\limits_{i=1}^n(x_i-\bar{x})^2}$
$=\sigma^2(\dfrac{1}{n}+\dfrac{\bar{x}^2}{\sum\limits_{i=1}^n(x_i-\bar{x})^2}).$
